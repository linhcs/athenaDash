  • Respiratory Issues: Increased risk of asthma, allergies, and bronchitis due to particulate matter and vehicle emissions.
  • Cardiovascular Strain: Prolonged exposure to high AQI can contribute to heart strain and related conditions.
  • Long-Term Health: Potential for chronic respiratory diseases with sustained exposure to poor air quality.
